About me

My passion for assisting others has driven me to excel in various industries especially education and technology.

I constantly seek ways to improve existing systems and processes and to put the 'extra' in extraordinary effort. I relish finding creative ways to overcome obstacles and solve problems, being challenged at work and constantly learning more.

My improving and expanding skill set has been used to train managers and lecture university students; negotiate with directors in boardrooms and train teams in meeting rooms; craft marketing strategies and consistently promote data-driven decision-making.

On a personal note, I enjoy spending time with family, being the "fun uncle" to my 9 young nephews and nieces, and travelling with my wife. I try to live a balanced lifestyle and surround myself with people who add value to it.
